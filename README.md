# AI Analytics Copilot

AI-powered analytics assistant designed to transform business questions into analytics insights.

---

## Project Objective

Build a modern analytics copilot capable of understanding natural language questions and assisting business decision making.

---

## Business Scenario

Business users often depend on analysts to retrieve and interpret data.

This project demonstrates an AI-assisted analytics workflow.

---

## Planned Features

- Natural Language Analytics
- Query Generation
- KPI Interpretation
- Business Recommendations
- Decision Support
